Who We Are

Affordable Care,
Without Compromise.

Multipoint Healthcare is a proudly Nigerian and pan-African healthcare solutions company. We exist to provide affordable and innovative medical equipment, devices, pharmaceuticals, and supplies to the last mile in the healthcare value chain — sourcing from global quality-certified manufacturers to meet the demands of Africa's 1.2 billion people.

With over a decade of hands-on industry experience, we partner with healthcare facilities of all sizes — from community clinics to major teaching hospitals — delivering the full spectrum of healthcare solutions.

  • Quality-certified equipment & consumables
  • Affordable, reasonably-priced products
  • Devoted after-sales support & maintenance
  • Consultative approach to every engagement
